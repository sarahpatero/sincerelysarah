A semester of momentum sprinkled with citrus.

Sarah

Published on January 11th, 2026

"Cheez-It Citrus Bowl in Orlando 2025"


"Never take your luck for granted. Always be grateful for EVERYTHING.


Closing the chapter on an incredible semester, and an even greater marching season. I took myself back and lived her out in full swing. I shattered the barriers that tantalized me from living my true self for so long, and now I truly feel like I'm living the perfect life for me.


I am proud of you beyond words, Sarah. You've come a long way. Never forget where you came from. I am so excited for your future and the amazing things you go on to achieve. You never fail to surprise me, and I am so crazy grateful to be you. Being you up until this point has been the opportunity of a lifetime. I have no words to thank you nearly enough.


Well, it's been real, Sarah. I love you dearly, Sarah " — January 1st, 2026


I handwrote this note to myself on the plane back from Orlando, Florida after spending 3 full days there with the Michigan Marching Band. I went to Universal Studios, Cocoa Beach, performed at Pointe Orlando, and had game day against Texas.


This semester felt like a clean slate, but not in the way that everything that happened prior was wiped clean. It was like all that had happened in my life up until that point built a launching pad to skyrocket onward and upward. The momentum in my life felt so forward in an exciting and curious way.


I shattered what I thought was possible with me and math, I channeled such a crazy confidence that I had been building starting in winter 2025, and I felt completely free in my spirit.


There was no better way to end off this life-changing semester than with the best bowl trip ever. The hotel was so luxurious. I tried my best to soak up everything on this trip like a sponge. I stayed out late. I took all the pictures I could. I was conscious in my breathing and taking in the present moment. I was genuinely so happy and so grateful to have gone on this trip.


So what now? Everything can only go up from here. I'm excited to keep surpassing what I believe is possible for myself.
