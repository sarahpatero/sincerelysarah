Ako ay pinagpala, salamat sa Diyos.

Sarah

Published on January 27th, 2026

How blessed I am to continue living out my passions. I am so incredibly grateful for this dance, for all my dancers, and for being a Filipino.


Especially, how grateful I am to be a Filipino. We are truly the happiest people on the planet. Filipinos are unapologetically themselves and we love each other to great depths. There is always such pride I notice that Filipinos take in our culture. We are so excited to meet a fellow Filipino. There is an undeniable bond and camaraderie that we are born with and forever sticks us together.


The times in my life where I've felt like I've belonged to a family THE most were always with other Filipinos. We love each other unconditionally and are truly there for each other. Thank you God for making me a Filipino. I could not have imagined a better life any other way!


Teaching this dance for so long as been one of my life's greatest honors. It is the perfect way to live out my true purpose: teaching people new things while encouraging self-confidence. That's all I've ever wanted in life: to have a real impact on someone's confidence. I am so blessed to see the growth of my dancers throughout the season. From them first saying to me "Sarah, I've never done this before" to them not even looking at the sticks while they dance for the final performance. The progress is always a delight to see; I'm happy to have been a part of it.


And the way teaching this dance pushes my own limits is inspiring. Looking at my own growth throughout my time teaching this dance really makes me proud of myself. From my first choreographed show at my high school to now, the level of skill that's been incorporated just shows how much better you yourself have gotten. Not only at teaching the dance, but at doing the dance yourself. I am so proud.


I'm thrilled to come back for one more year. My senior year tinikling show will be the best dance to ever grace FASA Umich.


All in all, I can't say it enough, I am truly blessed. Thank you God for how tinikling has shaped my life for the better.
