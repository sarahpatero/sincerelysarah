Nothing matters.

Sarah

Published on May 19th, 2025

A girl slacks off on her math homework and resorts to ChatGPT for the answers. "Nothing matters anwyays," she says. A boy throws a used tissue out his car window while he's driving. "Nothing matters,"" he thinks to himself.


Honestly, yea. On a cosmic scale, nothing truly matters. We are living on a floating rock in the middle of space. We are only a speck of nothingness on this Earth. Everything we see around us is a statistical accident. Nothing actually matters.


But a part of me dies when I hear people shrinking themselves and justifying it because "nothing matters." Like hello??? That's all the reason that you should be EXPANDING yourself into everything you've ever wanted to be.


Most people hear "nothing matters" and think: "Then why try?" But you don't want to be like most people. You want to hear "nothing matters" and think : "then why limit myself?"


Choosing smallness when nothing matters is a betrayal of life.


Why aren't you attacking the life that you want with full force and daring the become the person you've always wanted to be? If you want to be a hedon and only live life for pleasure, I know one of the greatest pleasures of life. There is no feeling more satisfying than watching yourself grow into the person you once dreamed of becoming, eudaimonia.
