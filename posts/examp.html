I passed Exam P!

Sarah

Published on January 29th, 2026

This matcha tasted a lot better after I found I passed Exam P.


There is no feeling more satisfying than watching yourself grow into the person you once dreamed of becoming. I am proud of this first step of many towards becoming an actuary.


Passing an exam like this was confirmation to myself that I can do ANYTHING I set my mind to. Going from getting a C+ in Calc 2, late-withdrawing from the Exam P prep class, to finally passing a year later was a gift in disguise. I'm proving to myself that I have grit and a vast show of determination.


This milestone reminds me that growth is rarely linear and that setbacks are the stepping stones to soon victories. Every challenge I've faced, every moment of doubt, has shaped the resilience and focus I carry today. I want to carry this mindset into everything I do. Not just exams, but life itself. Trust that perseverance, patience, and belief in myself will always lead me closer to the person I aspire to become.


So cheers to one down, and cheers to many, many, more.
