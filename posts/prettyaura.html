Pretty aura privilege.

Sarah

Published on June 8th, 2025

We feel each other before we see each other.

You’ve probably noticed how some people can walk into a room and instantly shift its atmosphere. Take someone who’s deeply worried—before they even speak, you can *feel* the weight they carry, even if they appear outwardly composed. Their energy enters the space before they do.

Because long before you even say a word, your energy has already arrived.

Have you ever seen someone so insanely gorgeous, looks like they have everything together, but you feel something uneasy about them? If you listen to your intuition closely, you can get a read on anyone, and it may contradict the surface-level version of them that you are viewing.

That’s how our aura speaks for us—through the silent language of energy. Your internal state is constantly being broadcast to the outside world, often picked up subconsciously by others, even if they aren’t fully aware of it. They might not be able to articulate what they’re sensing, but the impression they receive will inevitably reflect what you’re holding within.

Your inner energy is the most powerful magnetism you’ll ever possess.

I’m moving beyond the superficial belief that “looks are everything” or that beauty is a woman’s only currency. Yes, physical appearance can open doors—but that’s not the kind of legacy I want to leave behind. I want to create a deeper impact, one that lingers long after I’ve left the room. Not just to be seen, but to be felt. I want people to experience my presence from the inside out. I want to be unforgettable.

Your deepest magnetism doesn’t come from curating a perfect lifestyle—whatever that may mean to you. It’s far more profound than aesthetics or performance. True magnetism arises when you remember who you are at your core, and choose to live in alignment with that truth.

Cognitive dissonance: a psychological state of discomfort experienced when an individual holds two or more conflicting beliefs, attitudes, or values simultaneously. This conflict creates an unpleasant feeling of unease, and people are motivated to reduce this dissonance by altering their thoughts, behaviors, or environment to restore balance.

To point a finger at what exactly gives someone a pretty aura, it’s coherence with your true self.

One of my favorite podcasts “Ethereal Girl” in episode 53 touches on the formula: Pretty + Coherent + Receptive = Beauty as real world power. This is a fundamental truth regarding magnetism and attracting desires.

Breaking down each component:

Pretty: While external beauty can initially attract attention and turn heads, it’s not the sole or primary activation point for magnetism. The feminine energy is naturally meant to be seen and admired, so we need more than just being pretty.

Coherence: This refers to the state of your energetic field, particularly that emitted by your heart, which is the body’s most powerful magnetic field. When your nervous system is dysregulated, holding fear, shame, or insecurity, your field becomes “incoherent,” even if you appear outwardly beautiful. Coherence, on the other hand, means feeling safe in yourself and radiating self-acceptance, which emits a stable frequency and is described as true magnetism. An incoherent field distorts your signal and literally repels the abundance you seek, leading to a feeling of needing to try harder to earn things, which is ultimately unfulfilling. Your aura, vibration, and core feeling state – your coherence – is what the quantum field listens to and communicates with.

Receptivity: This speaks to your openness to receive without the belief that you must earn, suffer, or work hard for it. It involves allowing life to provide for you and allowing yourself to receive without the need to “do the most,” over-explain, over-give, overdo, or over-perform. It’s about remembering that you already are worthy and deserving, like the sun that doesn’t move toward the flower, but rather the flowers turn toward it. You are worthy simply because you are.

This formula results in “beauty as real world power,” which is defined as your energetic power and gift that does not have to be earned, but nurtured. It’s about remembering your natural state, like the ocean that simply is, without needing to explain or apologize.

Mastering this combination allows you to:

1. Draw in love, opportunity, and abundance effortlessly.

2. Hold people's attention, invite trust, and open hearts, moving beyond just turning heads.

3. Access magnetic energy by "returning to yourself" and stopping the performance or trying to earn what you already are.

4. Achieve genuine fulfillment and satisfaction, which requires creativity and connection—the signal you send to the universe.

5. Collapse timelines and attract your desires without needing to prove, chase, or convince.

6. Live a life of ease and pleasure rather than believing suffering is a prerequisite for wisdom, success, or spiritual knowledge. This "pretty aura privilege" means you don't need a "rags to riches" story to be magnetic, wise, or worthy; you are allowed to have lived a life of ease and receive even more ease.

7. Radiate an "algorithm" that allows you to express yourself freely and unapologetically, leading the world to adjust to you when you own this unapologetically.

We’ve all met people who look polished on the outside but carry an energy of tension, comparison, or deep shame. And we’ve met others who maybe don’t meet the world’s visual expectations—but their energy? Peaceful. Present. Whole. You feel it. That’s real power.

That coherence is what makes you magnetic. Your energy is your algorithm. It filters and attracts everything that enters your world. No strategy, aesthetic, or “glow-up” can override the frequency you emit.

When you feel at home within yourself, the world adjusts to you. Opportunities open. People respond. Not because you manipulated them—but because your coherence said, I trust myself to receive.

You no longer chase—you attract. Not from ego, but from embodiment.

Practices for Inner Magnetism
Here are a few gentle ways to return to your magnetic core:

Mirror work: Place your hand on your heart and say, “I don’t need to suffer to be worthy. I am safe to receive with ease.”

Nervous system safety: Take time to breathe slowly, move gently, and let your body unlearn the performance of proving.

Receptivity training: Let others help you. Let life surprise you. Practice saying “thank you” instead of “you didn’t have to.”

Visualize your aura: Imagine yourself surrounded by light. Not because you earned it—but because it’s your nature.

True magnetism is not about being liked, chosen, or praised. It’s about being felt in your fullness. Seen in your softness. Safe in your self-acceptance.
